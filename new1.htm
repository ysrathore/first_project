Beauty And Wellness

Beauty and wellness is a booming industry today thanks to growing awareness and desire among people to look good. An Assistant Beautician is expected to perform basic waxing, threading, manicure, pedicure, basic face care services and assist the Beautician in providing advanced services. She also assists in salon ambience maintenance and does various other odd jobs in the salon including advising clients on which salon products to buy
